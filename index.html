Institute Milestones

1999-2000:
College started as women’s engineering college with intake of 180 (4 branches – Instrumentation and control engineering, Computer engineering, Electronics engineering, Electrical engineering)

 

2001-2002:
Intake increased to 200

 

2002-2003:
First batch passed out

 

2003-2004:
First college magazine “Aayam”, First annual gathering “Shivostav”

 

2005-2006:
Inauguration of new building at the hands of Shri. Sharad Pawar

 

2006-2007:
Intake increased to 240 Conversion into coeducation

 

2007-2008:
Intake increased to 300 Started new branch – E&TC

 

2008-2009:
Started new branch – IT Intake increased to 360

 

2011-2012:
First National Level Conference on “Nanotechnology and its Applications in Electronics Engineering” sponsored by CSIR Student Ms. Prajkta Thakur secured rank in UPSC

 

2012-2013:
Intake increased to 480
First cycle of NBA accreditation of 3 UG programs.
First MoU signed with international university – Staffordshire University, United Kingdom
4 faculty members visited to Staffordshire University, United Kingdom
Permanent affiliation of four departments to SPPU

 

2013-2014:
Intake increased to 576
Started 2 PG programs in electrical and E&TC with intake of 18
Unveiling of statue of Rajshree Shahu Maharaj at the hands of the then President of India Shri. Pranab Mukherjee

Started research center in E&TC engineering
